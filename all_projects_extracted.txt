--- PROJECT 1 ---
Project 01 — Nuwaco RO Plant
Location:  Nuwaco, Puntland
Scale:  2,400 m³ per day
Service:  Design, installation and commissioning
The Nuwaco RO Plant is the largest water treatment facility delivered by ALNM Group. Serving the Nuwaco district of Puntland, the plant was designed, procured and commissioned entirely by the ALNM engineering team — from the initial site assessment and system design through to final handover and operational verification.
The plant processes 2,400 cubic metres of water per day through a multi-stage reverse osmosis treatment system, removing salinity, biological contaminants and suspended solids to deliver water that meets safe drinking standards. The installation includes pre-treatment filtration, high-pressure pump arrays with variable frequency drive control, multi-element RO membrane vessels, a chemical dosing system for antiscalant and biocide, a post-treatment mineralisation stage and a full PLC-based control and monitoring panel fabricated in-house by ALNM Engineering.
The project represents ALNM's complete service offering in water infrastructure — a single contractor responsible for survey, design, procurement, civil works, mechanical and electrical installation, commissioning and post-handover maintenance support.

--- PROJECT 5 ---
Project 05 — 300 kWp Solar Power System
Location:  Garowe, Puntland
Scale:  300 kWp installed
Service:  Full EPC — panels, inverters, DEIF solar-genset controllers, in-house panel fabrication
ALNM Energy and ALNM Engineering jointly delivered this 300 kWp solar power system in Garowe — one of the largest solar installations in Puntland to date. The project involved complete design, supply, installation and commissioning of the full photovoltaic array and associated electrical infrastructure.
The system was designed around high-efficiency monocrystalline solar panels in a ground-mount configuration, with three-phase string inverters feeding a common AC busbar. ALNM Engineering fabricated the complete solar-genset coordination control panel in-house, integrating a DEIF ASC 150 controller to manage the relationship between solar generation and the existing diesel generator set — automatically maximising solar utilisation and minimising generator run-hours.
The installation included all DC string cabling, DC surge protection, AC cable runs, earthing and bonding, and a remote monitoring connection providing live visibility of system performance. The completed system has operated reliably since commissioning with full manufacturer warranty support through ALNM's distributor relationship.

--- PROJECT 7 ---
Project 07 — Aqua Safi Bottling Facility
Location:  Garowe, Puntland
Scale:  10,000 bottles per hour production capacity
Service:  Facility design, procurement and installation
The Aqua Safi bottling facility is the manufacturing heart of ALNM Water's consumer brand — one of the largest bottled water production operations in the Horn of Africa. ALNM Group designed, procured and installed the complete production facility, from the water pre-treatment system through to the filling, capping and labelling line.
The production process begins with multi-stage RO water treatment to produce a consistent, high-purity feed. Treated water passes to the automated filling line, which operates at over 10,000 bottles per hour across the full product range: 250 ml, 500 ml, 1.5 L, 5 L, 10 L and 19 L formats. UV sterilisation protects water quality at the point of fill. The facility operates in a sealed, positive-pressure production environment.
The electrical installation covers the full facility power distribution, motor control for all production equipment, compressed air system for bottle handling, chilled water system for process cooling and the building management panel. ALNM Engineering fabricated the main distribution board and all sub-panels in-house.

--- PROJECT 10 ---
Project 10 — Industrial Hybrid Power System + SCADA
Location:  Puntland
Scale:  Solar + diesel generator + battery hybrid system
Service:  System design, full EPC, SCADA commissioning
This project represents ALNM Energy's most complex system architecture — a fully integrated hybrid power system combining solar generation, battery storage and diesel generator backup under a single energy management controller, with full SCADA remote monitoring commissioned by ALNM Digital.
The system design began with a detailed load analysis and energy audit of the facility, establishing the peak demand, base load and daily consumption profile. Solar array sizing was optimised against the site's irradiance data to maximise self-consumption, with battery storage sized to bridge the overnight and low-irradiance periods. The diesel generator provides backup for extended low-solar periods and peak demand events that exceed the combined solar-battery capacity.
ALNM Engineering fabricated the complete hybrid control panel in-house — integrating the solar inverter, battery management interface, automatic transfer switch, genset controller and energy management system communications bus. The SCADA platform, commissioned by ALNM Digital, provides the facility operator with live visibility of all energy flows, battery state of charge, generator status and system alarms via a web-based dashboard accessible from any location.
