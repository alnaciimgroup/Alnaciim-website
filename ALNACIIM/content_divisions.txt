Water Wells & Community Supply.

We handle the complete installation from the drill rig at the surface to the community water point — including power supply design and installation, storage tank procurement and installation, pipework and any distribution infrastructure required. Every well is handed over with a yield test result, water quality analysis, pump curve data and a recommended maintenance schedule.

SERVICE 02 — RO WATER TREATMENT
Reverse Osmosis Water Treatment.
ALNM Water designs and supplies reverse osmosis water treatment systems — from compact commercial units to large municipal and community plants. Our engineering team, operating through ALNM Engineering, handles system design, component supply, full mechanical and electrical installation, commissioning and ongoing maintenance.
We have delivered RO plants treating between 300 and 2,400 cubic metres of water per day across Puntland. Every plant is designed against the actual feed water chemistry of the source — salinity, hardness, suspended solids and biological load — to ensure the treatment train is correctly specified and the membranes perform at design capacity for their full rated service life.

SOLUTION 02 — HYBRID MICROGRID SYSTEMS
Hybrid Microgrid Design & Build.
A hybrid microgrid integrates solar generation, battery storage and a diesel or gas generator into a single managed power system. An intelligent energy management controller — programmed by ALNM Engineering — selects the optimal source in real time, maximising solar utilisation, using battery storage to bridge gaps and deploying the generator only when necessary.
ALNM Energy designs these systems from first principles. Our engineers determine the correct solar array capacity, battery size and generator rating for each site and load profile, select the appropriate hybrid controller, and supervise the complete installation and commissioning through ALNM Engineering. The result is a power system that reduces fuel cost, reduces emissions and increases supply reliability in a single installation.
Applications
Off-grid homes, villas and residential compounds
Hospitals, clinics and healthcare facilities without grid access
Schools, community buildings and social infrastructure
Telecom towers and remote communications infrastructure
Industrial sites and manufacturing facilities seeking to reduce diesel dependency
Agricultural operations, irrigation pump stations and aquaculture facilities
Components we supply and integrate
High-efficiency monocrystalline solar panels
Hybrid inverters with solar, battery, grid and generator management capability
LFP battery storage systems — sized for the overnight and low-irradiance load
Diesel generator sets — correctly rated for the peak demand and backup duration required

SOLUTION 03 — BATTERY ENERGY STORAGE
Battery Energy Storage.
LFP battery storage systems for residential, commercial and industrial applications. ALNM Energy supplies, specifies and commissions battery storage solutions across the full capacity range — from a 5 kWh home system installed alongside a rooftop solar array to a containerised multi-MWh commercial BESS project.
We specify LFP (lithium iron phosphate) chemistry exclusively for primary storage applications. LFP offers superior cycle life, thermal stability and safety compared to older battery chemistries, and the leading LFP manufacturers now produce systems rated for over 6,000 cycles at 80% depth of discharge — equivalent to more than 16 years of daily cycling.

We develop HMI screens, data historian configurations, alarm management systems and remote access dashboards — working directly with ALNM Engineering on all Group projects and accepting direct commissions from external industrial clients.
What we deliver
SCADA system design — architecture, communications, data point definition, alarm philosophy
HMI development — operator touchscreen panels and web-based supervisor interfaces
Data historian — operational data recorded at required resolution, accessible for analysis
Alarm management — prioritised alarm list, suppression rules, notification routing
Remote monitoring portal — secure web access to live plant data from any location
PLC data integration — connection to existing Siemens, Allen-Bradley, Schneider PLCs
RTU integration — Modbus, DNP3, IEC 61850 and proprietary protocol translation
Cybersecurity — OT network segmentation, access control and security audit
Training and handover — operator training, system documentation, support contracts
ALNM Digital uses the Ignition SCADA platform by Inductive Automation for all new industrial SCADA projects — an industry-standard, web-native platform that runs in any browser without client software installation.
